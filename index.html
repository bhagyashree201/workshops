G+4 Commercial Building Design Workshop

MIDAS R&D CENTRE

“It’s not the beauty of a building you should look at; it's the construction of the foundation that will stand the test of time” - David Allan Coe

Megalith in collaboration with MIDAS R&D CENTRE is set to organize the workshops which will present an opportunity to learn from the professionals with years of experience in technology and management of Building Design. This gives MIDAS the right pedigree to provide work specific comprehensive consultancy and quality education. This workshop on commercial building design will use MIDAS Gen software and will aim to familiarize participants of exhaustive analysis, design, and drafting of a solution for a variety of building and general structures.

To know more about "MIDAS Gen" software, Click Here!

Benefits

  • The major objective of the workshop is to practically train participants about the design and analysis of complex and large structures.
  • Certificate of participation from Midas R&D Centre & Megalith, IIT Kharagpur

Duration

The workshop will be conducted by MIDAS R&D CENTRE on 9th March 2022. A one day workshop comprising of 2 sessions of 3 hours to be provided on MIDAS Gen to the participants with a hands-on session on theory as well as practical problems.

Building Information Modelling Workshop

Trimble India Pvt. Ltd.

This workshop on Building Information Modeling (BIM) will use Tekla Structures software and will aim to familiarize participants with the application of the software in various Structural Engineering work.
